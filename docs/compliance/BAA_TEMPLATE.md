# Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

**Between:**
**Inner Infinite Power™ LLC (“Business Associate”)**
and
**[Covered Entity Name] (“Covered Entity”)**

---

## 1. Purpose
This Business Associate Agreement (“Agreement”) ensures compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA), the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act (HITECH), and related regulations governing the handling of Protected Health Information (“PHI”).

---

## 2. Definitions
- **PHI:** Individually identifiable health information as defined in 45 CFR §160.103.
- **Business Associate:** Inner Infinite Power™ LLC, operating the IIP_SECURE_STACK platform and associated AI-driven wellness systems.
- **Covered Entity:** The organization contracting with Inner Infinite Power™ LLC for services involving PHI.
- **Services:** Data analytics, automation, workflow integration, and AI-enabled transformation services supporting health and wellness delivery.

---

## 3. Obligations of Business Associate
1. **Safeguards:**
   IIP™ shall implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards that reasonably and appropriately protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of PHI, in accordance with 45 CFR §164.306.
2. **Use and Disclosure:**
   PHI shall only be used or disclosed as permitted by this Agreement or required by law.
3. **Incident Response:**
   Any known or suspected data breach or unauthorized disclosure of PHI must be reported to the Covered Entity within **72 hours** of discovery.
4. **Subcontractors:**
   IIP™ ensures that all subcontractors who receive PHI agree in writing to the same restrictions and conditions.
5. **Access and Accounting:**
   IIP™ will make available PHI in compliance with 45 CFR §§164.524 and 164.528.
6. **Audit Cooperation:**
   Upon request, IIP™ shall provide system logs, configuration evidence, and security reports necessary for the Covered Entity to demonstrate HIPAA compliance.
7. **Training:**
   All IIP™ personnel handling PHI will complete HIPAA awareness and cybersecurity training annually.

---

## 4. Permitted Uses and Disclosures
IIP™ may use PHI only for the following purposes:
- To provide contracted data management, analytics, and transformation services.
- For de-identified data aggregation, consistent with 45 CFR §164.514(a) and (b).
- For internal operations necessary to maintain compliance and improve security posture.

---

## 5. Term and Termination
This Agreement remains in effect until terminated by either Party with **30 days’ written notice**.
Upon termination, IIP™ will return or securely destroy all PHI, except where retention is required by applicable law.
Destruction certificates shall be provided to the Covered Entity within 15 business days of confirmation.

---

## 6. Liability and Indemnification
Each Party agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other Party, its officers, employees, and agents from any claims, damages, or losses arising from its failure to comply with this Agreement or applicable laws.
Liability shall be limited to direct damages except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.
